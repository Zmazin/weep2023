ABOUT BAND
& CONCEPT

A passionate emotional hardcore band
with themes of life's regrets, progress,
change, and self-seeking goals.

INTRODUCTION

In 2023, the five-member band "WEEP"
suddenly appeared in the hardcore scene.
In June of the same year, they released their first album
"The attack before the first light".
They started live performances in May of the same year.
With their overwhelming sense of melody and accelerated speed The band continues to be at the forefront of the hardcore scene with their one-of-a-kind, world-view-filled live performances. They continue to be active in the hardcore scene.
